Condition of participation

To take part in the competition of short films "One shot" is very simple. You need to choose one of the stories on our website or come up with your own. In fact, you have complete freedom of creativity, and the only limitation is that your film should be made one-shot. Thus, you need to make only five steps:

  • choose or come up with a story;
  • assemble a team of 2 people;
  • take one frame from 30 to 180 seconds;
  • upload video to some file sharing storage;
  • send a request with a link to the video.

Three stories for your film:

Plot 1. The teacher asked the children to solve the example and gave them a sheet on which they wrote their names. When one of pupils (for example, James) got distracted, the other (for example, Simon) swapped the sheets. As a result, James carefully solved the example on the sheet with the name of Simon on it while Simon was having a rest. Then they handed over the sheets to the teacher, who told the diligent James that his work got a bad mark, but Simon did a great job.

Plot 2. The child played too much with his phone. When his classmates called him, he always said that he would come soon, but he never came. And only after he was called for the fourth time, he asked: - Well, why did you call me? His classmates replied that nothing urgent, because they had already participated in the competition at the birthday party and won some prizes ate the cake. (There are options for the final. You have complete freedom of creativity.)

Plot 3. Children are running somewhere, and a little girl asks: "Where are you going?" They answer:" Run, there Jack Brown is coming! "The child looks to see this terrifying Jack Brown and suddenly sees the big boy with a terrible look. But the child overcomes her fear and does not run away. When this big guy approaches her, the child suddenly gets up in a fighting rack. The big boy jumps back and says, "What are you doing?" I'm not afraid of you, Jack Brown. The guy is asking: "What Jack Brown?". And then comes a happy little boy and quietly says: "It's me. Did you see where they went?". And then loudly: "Try to find us, we won’t quit, Start to count, because you’re “It!".

Specifications

It is necessary to make a single-frame film and send an application for participation from March 1 to 30, 2019.

In the absence of titles in Your video, the contest organizers add them at the beginning of Your film in accordance with the application for participation in the contest, so knowledge of the programs for editing is not required.

If you want to make the titles yourself keep in mind that the timing of the work should not exceed 180 seconds, and the video format should remain the same as in the original video, or 16:9 with a resolution of 1280 by 720 pixels to 4k.

It does not matter whether your film is shot on a phone, camera or professional camera - the main thing is to hold it horizontally and avoid unnecessary shaking. Videos with vertical frame location, containing scenes of violence, obscene language, infringing copyright, sent to the organizers in an unspecified time, will not be accepted for participation in the contest.

All works that meet the conditions of the competition will be published on YouTube and available on the website of the short film competition "One-shot" at ok.ideafilm.ru April 1, 2019.

How to come up with your story?

You can first assemble a team of potential actors and cameramen and brainstorm to come up with one or more plots. Any story can be divided into three components:

  • Intriguing tie is designated as the main conflict.
  • Plot development shows attempts to resolve the conflict.
  • An unexpected outcome shows how the conflict was resolved.

Don't forget the application to specify who came up with the plot. If you are using someone's story or story, do not violate copyright. For example, write that it's based on such works.
